Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy is of critical importance to us. Las Services (hereinafter "the Bot") has established this Privacy Policy to explain what information we process, how we utilize and store that information, and how server administrators and users can exercise control over their personal records.

By adding the Bot to your server or interacting with its features, you consent to the processing patterns detailed in this document.

2. Information Collected

To perform its operations, the Bot processes standard structured metadata transmitted through the official Discord API. This data includes:

  • User Identifiers: Discord User IDs, usernames, and avatar hashes to identify users creating support tickets or performing administrative tasks.
  • Guild Identifiers: Guild (Server) IDs and Role IDs to manage permissions, server configuration mappings, and log channels.
  • Channel Identifiers: Ticket channel IDs, log channel IDs, and message IDs to direct ticket threads and update automated logs.
  • Message & Support Log Content: Only text messages sent inside specific active ticket channels or logs. The Bot does NOT record or scan general, private, or off-ticket conversations.
  • Action Timestamps: Timestamps associated with ticket creation, message updates, moderations, or command triggers for analytical logging.

3. How Information Is Used

The processed information is utilized strictly to provide, maintain, and support the requested services:

  • Providing ticket handling operations, routing transcripts, and forwarding support messages to active ticket channels.
  • Enforcing server-specific moderation parameters, auto-moderation rules, and logging action history as defined by guild admins.
  • Debugging internal errors, tracking rate-limiting triggers, and optimizing response latency.
  • Ensuring security and preventing abusive spam campaigns or unauthorized API commands.

4. Data Storage and Retention

We take data retention seriously and store only what is functionally necessary:

  • Server Configurations: Saved in secure cloud-hosted configurations for as long as the Bot remains in your guild. If you kick the Bot, this configuration can be purged upon request.
  • Ticket Transcripts: Saved securely inside our logs database. By default, transcripts are deleted after 30 days unless a longer or shorter retention period is configured by the server administration.
  • Temporary Caching: User names and role lists are cached in memory for ephemeral command execution and automatically discarded shortly after.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical measures to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. All communication between our backend hosting server and the Discord API is encrypted in transit using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL/TLS).

Access to server databases and configurations is restricted to authorized personnel strictly for development and critical debugging tasks.

6. Third-Party Services

Our infrastructure communicates with the Discord API. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your server details, user IDs, or ticket transcripts to outside third parties, advertisers, or analytics brokers.

Please note that your interaction with the Bot is also subject to the Discord Privacy Policy, which controls overall account telemetry and data processing within the Discord client application.

7. Cookies

This landing website is a static information resource. It does not utilize trackable advertising cookies, pixel tags, or long-term browser storage scripts.

8. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You hold total authority over your data. Under standard regulations (including GDPR and CCPA), you have the right to request access to, correction of, or permanent deletion of your data:

  • Guild Purges: Guild owners can request full deletion of their server configurations and transcripts by submitting an inquiry to support.
  • Individual Deletion: Users can request details about stored ticket logs containing their ID and prompt a purge.

To request data deletion, please join our Discord support guild or send an email through our primary administration channels.

9. Policy Updates

We may occasionally modify this Privacy Policy to align with new system enhancements, Discord guidelines, or legal compliance. When changes are made, the revision date at the top of this document will be updated. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to file a data deletion request, please reach out through our official Discord community server.
